Wonder of the World Project

A showcase of a select Old, New, and Natural Wonders of the World! The point of this project was to experiement with different art forms while also capitalizing on the unique advantages they each provide. This was a project done for AP Art and Design and recieved a 5 by College Board.

The Colosseum - New Wonder - Charcoal
The Great Barrier Reef - Natural Wonder - Color Pencil
The Great Wall of China - New Wonder - Chinese Ink
Mount Everest - Natural Wonder - Gouache
The Taj Mahal - New Wonder - Acrylic
The Great Pyramids of Giza - Old Wonder - Chalk Pastel
The Northern Lights - Natural Wonder - Oil Pastel
Lighthouse of Alexandria - Old Wonder - Watercolor and Pen Mixed Media
Petra - New Wonder - Pointillism
