Austria Textile Project

Our final consisted of reconstructing typographic found objects to recreate a repeatable pattern. Using Illustrator and Photoshop, we needed to create our own abstracted design that could be used to screen print different final results.

Course: Color Pattern Textile

Term: Summer 2023

Chosen Project

I choose to draw four different flowers, each representing one of the four states where my family lives: Massachusetts, Florida, Alaska, and Colorado. Once these flowers were drawn, stencil is made on a screen, either by applying a light-sensitive emulsion and exposing it with a transparency or by directly applying a blocking material to the mesh.

Halfway done to final project.
Final Project: A banner organized with frammed windows to represent looking into the different phases of life my family has been through: utilizing color, shape, and pattern.
